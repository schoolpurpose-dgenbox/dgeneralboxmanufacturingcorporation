Who We Are

D' General Box Manufacturing Corporation is a local manufacturing firm specializing in the production of high-quality corrugated packaging solutions. The company primarily serves various sectors requiring durable and customizable packaging for shipping, storage, and branding. Known for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, D' General Box caters to both small and large-scale business needs across Cebu and nearby provinces.

Our Mission

To provide eco-friendly, innovative, and durable packaging solutions while upholding the highest standards of customer service and environmental responsibility.
